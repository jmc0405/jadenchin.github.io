About Me

Jaden Chin - Data Science and Economics

Welcome to my personal website! My name is Jaden Chin and I am a third-year student at Northeastern University, double majoring in Data Science and Economics. My academic journey has taken an interesting path — I originally started as a Civil Engineering and Computer Science student before pivoting to Data Science and Economics, a shift driven by my growing interest in how data and markets shape the world around us. That curiosity for the built environment never fully went away, and today it informs how I think about real estate, infrastructure, and financial systems. During my co-op at Wayfair, I built automated performance marketing trackers and conducted data analysis using Python and SQL, giving me hands-on experience turning raw data into actionable business insights. Outside of coursework, I serve as Director of Commodities at Northeastern's Global Markets Association, where I lead a team of analysts producing investment research on markets including uranium, copper, gold, and oil. I'm also the creator of a Python-based real estate analysis tool that models rent-to-price ratios and cash flow across U.S. markets using Zillow data. I'm actively seeking co-op and internship opportunities at the intersection of data and finance — particularly in fintech, financial analytics, and proptech. When I'm not working with data, you'll find me DJing, snowboarding, or exploring whatever city I happen to be in.
