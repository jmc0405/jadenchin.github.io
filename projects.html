Projects & Work

Real Estate Analysis Tool

I created a Python-based real estate analysis tool that models rent-to-price ratios and cash flow across U.S. markets using Zillow data. The tool allows users to input property details and market data to generate insights on potential rental income, expenses, and overall investment viability. It incorporates factors such as local market trends, property characteristics, and financial metrics to help users make informed decisions about real estate investments. The tool is designed to be user-friendly and accessible for both novice and experienced investors looking to analyze the profitability of rental properties.

Performance Marketing Trackers at Wayfair

During my co-op at Wayfair, I built automated performance marketing trackers and conducted data analysis using Python and SQL. These trackers were designed to monitor key performance indicators (KPIs) for various marketing campaigns, providing real-time insights into campaign effectiveness and ROI. By automating the data collection and reporting process, I enabled the marketing team to quickly identify trends, optimize campaigns, and make data-driven decisions to enhance overall marketing performance. The experience gave me hands-on exposure to working with large datasets and translating raw data into actionable business insights.
